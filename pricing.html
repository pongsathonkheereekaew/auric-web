FAQ

Common questions

What payment methods do you accept?

Gumroad processes payments securely. All major credit and debit cards are accepted, including Visa, Mastercard, and Amex, plus PayPal.

Do I need an internet connection to use Auric?

No. Auric is fully offline, including activation — your license key is validated locally by its signature, with no network call at any point.

What are the limits of the free Trial?

The Trial is free with no time limit, capped at 300 samples in your library. Activate Personal to remove the cap and unlock the full library workflow — your library and settings carry over. Create Variation and the batch power-tools are not in this release; they are planned for a future update.

Can I use my license on more than one machine?

Yes. Your key activates offline and is portable — paste it on any machine you personally work on. There's no machine binding and no activation count; we ask only that you keep it to your own honor-system fair use.

Who handles payment and tax?

Gumroad is our merchant of record. They process the payment and collect and remit any applicable VAT or sales tax. Your receipt and invoice come from Gumroad.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day refund window from the date of purchase. Contact hello@easbystudios.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5 business days via Gumroad.

14-day money-back guarantee. If Auric doesn't fit your workflow, email hello@easbystudios.com within 14 days of purchase for a full refund — no questions asked.
